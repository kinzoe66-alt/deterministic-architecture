# READOUT_ASSEMBLY_CONTRACT

Purpose:
Define the immutable order and role of system readouts.

Order (non-negotiable):
1. Structural Summary
2. Tactical Read
3. Operational Read
4. Executive Read

Rules:
- Sections may not be reordered
- Sections may not be merged
- Sections may not reference future sections
- Higher levels may contextualize lower levels, never override

Section Roles:
- Structural Summary: system state snapshot
- Tactical Read: immediate framing constraints
- Operational Read: sequencing and stability logic
- Executive Read: irreversible system implications

Notes:
This contract governs assembly only.
Content remains schema-derived.
